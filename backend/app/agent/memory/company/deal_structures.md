# Bluff Deal Structures

## Standard VIP Packages

These are Bluff's standard deal templates by tier. Actual deals may vary based on negotiation.

### Whale Package ($10M+ monthly)
- Lossback: Up to 30% of GGR
- Reload: 10% of GGR
- Level-up: 5% of GGR
- Weekly: 3% of GGR
- Monthly: 7% of GGR
- Affiliate: 25% of NGR
- Total bonus load: ~55% of GGR

### Dolphin Package ($5M monthly)
- Same rate structure as Whale
- Lower absolute amounts due to smaller volume

### Lower Tiers (Tuna, Bass, Sardine)
- Reduced lossback rates available
- Same percentage structure but discretionary adjustments possible

## Deal Negotiation Levers

When structuring custom deals, the key levers are:
1. **Lossback rate** — Most impactful. Reducing from 30% to 10-15% can flip a deal from loss to profit
2. **Reload rate** — Second largest bonus cost
3. **Affiliate commission** — Negotiable per deal
4. **Wager mix requirements** — Can require minimum sportsbook or low-RTP play

## Revenue Share (Affiliate Deals)

Progressive revenue share tiers based on total monthly NGR:
- Rates start at base and increase with volume
- At $50M monthly wagers, effective rev share is ~7.25%
