# Bonus Mechanics

## Bonus Types (VIP Model)

All bonus rates are expressed as a percentage of GGR unless noted.

### Level-Up Bonus (Default: 5% of GGR)
- One-time reward when a player reaches a new VIP level
- Incentivizes continued play to reach the next tier
- Fixed dollar amounts per level in the 20-level structure ($0 at L1 up to $25K at L14, then $0 for L15+)

### Reload Bonus (Default: 10% of GGR)
- Ongoing bonus on deposits/reloads
- Encourages players to keep depositing
- One of the larger recurring costs

### Weekly Bonus (Default: 3% of GGR)
- Recurring weekly reward based on activity
- Keeps players engaged on a weekly cadence

### Monthly Bonus (Default: 7% of GGR)
- Larger recurring monthly reward
- Tied to monthly wager volume achievements

### Lossback / Standard (Default: 30% of GGR)
- Returns a percentage of losses to the player
- **The single largest cost item** in the VIP P&L
- Highly sensitive — changing from 30% to 10% dramatically improves margins

### Lossback / Discretionary (Default: 0%)
- Additional lossback given at management discretion
- Used for retention of high-value players
- Stacks on top of standard lossback

### Cashback (20-Level Structure Only)
- Available at Level 10+ (5% at L10-14, 10% at L15, 12% at L16-17, 15% at L18-20)
- Applied as a percentage of losses
- Replaces lossback at higher tiers

## Bonus Stacking

All bonuses stack additively. Total bonus cost = sum of all individual bonuses.

Default total: 5% + 10% + 3% + 7% + 30% = **55% of GGR** goes to bonuses.

## Deposit Match Promo (Optional)
- Toggle-able in the calculator
- Matches a % of player deposits up to a cap
- Subject to rollover requirements (default 20x)
- Uses deposit-to-wager ratio (default 8x) to convert

## Wager Milestone Promo (Optional)
- Triggers at a wager volume target (default $500K)
- Can be based on nominal or weighted volume
- Reward as % of target volume

## 20-Level VIP Bonus Structure

The full VIP program has 20 levels from $1M to $500M in wager thresholds. Effective bonus cost % of GGR ranges from ~21% at Level 1 to ~43% at Level 20. The cost escalation comes primarily from cashback kicking in at L10+ and increasing at higher levels.

## Company P&L Bonus Phases

In the company P&L model, bonus rates vary by company maturity:
- **Phase 1 (Months 1-4)**: 29.2% of GGR — acquisition phase, competitive bonuses
- **Phase 2 (Months 5-8)**: 31.2% of GGR — growth phase
- **Phase 3 (Months 9+)**: 37.8% of GGR — mature phase, higher retention costs
